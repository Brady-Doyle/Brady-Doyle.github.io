The Story Behind this Website

The idea behind this website was born where all great ideas are born: in the shower. Well, kind of. I began creating this website just to learn how to. But I had to find a purpose for the site, so I decided to make the site for what everyone does when creating a website, and make it a personal portfolio. I created the whole original website around this purpose. Eventually I realized that I probably wouldn't be able to create a better portfolio than LinkedIn can and I quickly lost motivation. I then took some time away from working on this site but one day in the shower I was thinking about what people will be missing in the near to mid future. What I thought of was something that we are already struggling with which is the increasing lack of being about to decipher between AI and humans. This is especially concerning when considering ideas that are conveyed through writing. It is increasingly difficult to tell if a block of text was created by humans or AI without running it through an AI checker. This is when I thought of the idea to change my website to have the purpose of hosting peoples ideas while ensuring they are HUMAN ideas. So there, that is the idea of this website. Although you may be realizing that there is nowhere on this site that indicates that the articles are purely written by humans or any AI checking. That is because there isn't. I decided that in the early days, I will keep this site as open to any articles; no restricitions. When/if the website grows, I will begin to enact this purpose in the website. This will hopefully create a safe place for humans to share and consume ideas that they can be confident were created by fellow humans.
